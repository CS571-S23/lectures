Equipment

Our alley comes equipped with...

  • Lanes: The playing surface where the game is played.
  • Pins: The targets that the bowler tries to knock down.
  • Bowling Balls: The ball used to play the game.
  • Shoes: Special shoes worn by bowlers that have a slippery sole to prevent skidding.
  • Automatic Scoring System: An electronic system that keeps track of the score.
  • Pin Setting Machine: A machine that automatically sets the pins back up after each frame.
